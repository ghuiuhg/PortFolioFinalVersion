Presentation of bts sio :


The BTS (Computer Services for Organisations) is a state-recognised diploma recognized by the state and of level BAC +2 This BTS gives us many qualifications in both programming and networking. This BTS offers two specialities such as: The BTS SIO Option SISR and the BTS SIO Option SLAM.


Discover the two options of the BTS SIO :

- SISR Option (System and Network Infrastructure Solutions) )The SISR option enables us to train for jobs related to the design and maintenance of network infrastructures. and maintenance of network infrastructures. It ensures the security, maintenance maintenance and installation of networks and equipment which are It ensures the security, maintenance and installation of networks and equipment, which are part of the main missions of future technical administrators or operating pilots.

- SLAM Option (Software Solutions and Business Applications) SLAM option allows us to train for jobs related to the design and maintenance of application programs. The graduates will be able to be able to manage a computer park or administer a network within a company within a company or as a self-employed person.
